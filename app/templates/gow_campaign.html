How it works

This builds one console script that creates the whole Game-of-the-Week promo: the journey draft (free-spin offer with 4 deposit tiers) and the promo page wired to it, with the campaign photo uploaded into every visual slot.

The per-tier free-spin bet comes from Bet values (ascending by deposit tier). The free-spin claim window is always the start date + 7 days.
